# Compliance And Responsible Use Notes

Users are responsible for complying with applicable email marketing, privacy, consumer protection, and anti-spam laws in every jurisdiction where they operate.

This starter kit:

- does not send emails by default;
- does not include Gmail, Google Sheets, or delivery integrations;
- is designed for research, planning, review, and workflow design;
- should not be used to automate large-scale unsolicited messaging.

Responsible workflow reminders:

- Review leads before outreach.
- Use public and lawful data sources.
- Avoid sensitive, regulated, or inappropriate categories unless you have qualified legal guidance.
- Always include opt-out language where outreach is allowed.
- Stop contacting anyone who replies, bounces, unsubscribes, or is manually marked as stopped.
- Keep private credentials and personal data out of public repositories.

When adapting this starter kit into a client project, document the legal basis, review process, data retention policy, and stop-contact workflow before sending any messages.
